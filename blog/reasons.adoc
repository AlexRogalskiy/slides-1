=== Why should I blog?

*Because you want to!*

=== Why may I want to?

Goal oriented:

* you want to build a brand
* you want to become a speaker, author, ...
* you want to sell books, courses, apps, ...

=== Why may I want to?

Process oriented:

* you like teaching
* you like sharing
* you like writing
